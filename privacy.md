# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 8, 2026**

## Overview

Modulo is a personal savings tracking app. This privacy policy explains how Modulo handles your data.

**The short version: Modulo collects no personal data. Everything stays on your device.**

## Data We Do Not Collect

Modulo does not collect, store, or share any of the following:

- Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
- Financial account information or banking credentials
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics or crash reports sent to our servers
- Any other personally identifiable information

## How Your Data Is Stored

All data you enter in Modulo — savings goals, contributions, and preferences — is stored **locally on your device**. If you have iCloud enabled, your data is synced across your own Apple devices via iCloud. This sync is handled entirely by Apple and is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

At no point does your data transit through our servers or become accessible to us.

## Third-Party Services

Modulo does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with any third party.

## Children's Privacy

Modulo does not collect any data from any user, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised date at the top.
